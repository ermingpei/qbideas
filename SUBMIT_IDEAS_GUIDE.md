## ğŸ“ Submission Process

### Step 1: Sign Up / Login
You must be authenticated to submit ideas.
- Create an account at `/signup`
- Or login at `/login`

### Step 2: Fill Out the Form
The submission wizard includes:

#### Basic Information
- **Title** (10-200 characters)
  - Clear, descriptive name for your idea
  - Example: "AI-Powered Recipe Generator for Dietary Restrictions"

- **Description** (50-5000 characters)
  - Detailed explanation of your idea
  - What problem it solves
  - How it works
  - Target audience

- **Category**
  - Choose from: SaaS, Mobile App, Web App, Chrome Extension, API Service, Marketplace, Productivity, Social, E-commerce, Fintech, Healthtech, Edtech, DevTools, AI/ML, Other

#### Problem & Solution
- **Problem Statement** (20-1000 characters)
  - What problem does this solve?
  - Why is it important?
  - Who experiences this problem?

- **Target Audience** (10-500 characters)
  - Who will use this?
  - Demographics, industries, or user types
  - Market size estimate

- **Proposed Solution** (20-2000 characters)
  - How does your idea solve the problem?
  - Key features and functionality
  - Unique value proposition

### Step 3: Submit for Review
- Click "Submit Idea"
- Your idea enters the evaluation queue
- You'll receive a submission confirmation

### Step 4: AI Evaluation
Your idea is automatically evaluated on:

#### Scoring Criteria (0-1 scale)
1. **Market Potential** (0-1)
   - Market size and demand
   - Revenue potential
   - Growth opportunities

2. **Technical Feasibility** (0-1)
   - Implementation complexity
   - Technology requirements
   - Development timeline

3. **Innovation** (0-1)
   - Uniqueness and originality
   - Competitive advantage
   - Market differentiation

4. **Overall Score** (0-1)
   - Weighted average of all criteria
   - Determines approval/rejection

### Step 5: Review Results
Within **24 hours**, you'll receive:

#### If Approved âœ…
- Your idea is published to the marketplace
- Assigned a tier (Regular or Premium)
- Visible to all users
- Starts earning revenue when unlocked

#### If Rejected âŒ
- Detailed feedback on why
- Suggestions for improvement
- Option to revise and resubmit

### Don'ts âŒ
1. **Don't Be Vague**
   - Avoid generic descriptions

   - Specify your unique angle

2. **Don't Copy**
   - No plagiarism
   - Original ideas only
   - Cite inspirations if any

   - Be honest about challenges
   - Realistic market estimates
   - Acknowledge competition

4. **Don't Rush**
   - Take time to refine
   - Proofread carefully
   - Get feedback before submitting

5. **Don't Spam**
   - Max 5 submissions per day
   - Quality over quantity
   - Each idea should be unique
